Panthograph

A pantograph is a mechanical linkage that is coupled in a parallelogram-based fashion so that the movement of one pen while tracing an image causes identical motions in a second pen. If a line drawing is traced by the first point, a pen attached to the other will create an identical, expanded, or smaller replica.
